About ClassroomGS

ClassroomGS is a free game platform built by Derrick Richard to give all people a fun, safe place to have fun. With over 600 games spanning arcade, puzzle, multiplayer, and educational genres, it's designed for quick access and endless entertainment. no logins, no hassle. Whether you're taking a break or sharpening your skills, ClassroomGS is your go-to destination for classroom-friendly gaming.

Suggest a Game to Add

Want to see your favorite game on ClassroomGS? Choose how you'd like to contribute:

  • Share Game Files: Already have the game? Upload it and we’ll take care of the rest.
  • Request a Game: Tell us the name and we’ll try to track it down and add it.
  • Custom Game Request: Have a cool idea? Let’s build something awesome together!

Your game is guaranteed to be added as long as it meets all the terms of service, which are outlined in the submission form.
